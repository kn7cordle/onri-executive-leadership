Program Overview

This accelerated course delivers applied leadership training, ethical decision-making, executive presence development, and resilience-based performance strategies aligned with workforce standards.

Program Learning Outcomes

Graduates will demonstrate ownership of decisions, communicate with executive presence, lead ethically under pressure, resolve conflict professionally, apply structured decision-making, model integrity and resilience, and execute leadership responsibilities in real-world environments.

Core Leadership Framework (ONRÍ Model)

OwnershipFull accountability and results ownership
NobilityProfessional dignity and principled leadership
ResilienceHigh performance under adversity
IntegrityEthical consistency in all decision-making
